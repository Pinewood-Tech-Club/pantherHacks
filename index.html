Pinewood Hackathon

PantherHacks:

fjkdlsj The tech club at Pinewood is hosting their annual hackathon. This hackathon will take place on November 4th, from 10:00AM - 8:00PM and November 5th, from 10:00AM - 6:00PM. We hope to see you there!

Experience Requirements:

Absolutely no programming experience is required to participate in the Pinewood hackathon. It is recommended that you submit your experience on the form here to help us gauge the skill of everyone participating in the hackathon.

What To Bring:

You need to bring your own laptop for this hackathon. Food will NOT be provided. You need to bring your own lunch! There are water fountains at the school campus, so there is no need to bring your own waterbottle.

Prizes:

The prizes for this hackathon, other than the friends you made along the way, will be distributed based on how fun your game is, how well it fits the theme, if it implements the limitation, and how much experience you have.
