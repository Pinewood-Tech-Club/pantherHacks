Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

Absolutely no programming experience is required to participate in PantherHacks. However, each person is required to submit their experience while signing up.

What do I need to bring?

  • Laptop, charger and snacks!

Who can participate?

While some schools have been explicitly invited to PantherHacks, any middle and high schooler in the Bay Area Peninsula is welcome to participate!
PantherHacks spots fill up quickly, so claim yours now!

How will my project be scored?

PantherHacks will be providing a theme and different prize catagories, so projects are scored based on how well they incorporate both of these elements. Additionally, projects will be scored on technical difficulty, challenge yourself!
