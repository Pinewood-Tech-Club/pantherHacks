Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

Absolutely no programming experience is required to participate in PantherHacks. It is recommended that you submit your experience on the form here to help us gauge the skill of everyone participating in the hackathon.

What do I need to bring?

  • Your own Laptop and charger
  • Snacks, Lunch will be provided
  • There are water fountains on Campus, so there is no need to bring a waterbottle

Who can participate

While some schools have been explicitly invited to PantherHacks, any school in the Bay Area Penninsula is welcome to participate!

How will my project be scored?

PantherHacks will be providing a theme and different prize catagories, so projects are scored based on how well they incorporate both of these elements. Additionally, projects will be scored on technical difficulty, challenge yourself!
