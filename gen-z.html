Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?

No need to be a coding whiz to join PantherHacks, but when you sign up, we do wanna know your experience level. 🙌💻📝

What do I need to bring?

  • Make sure to bring your own laptop and charger, don't forget the snacks! We've got water fountains on campus, so no need to haul around a water bottle. #YOLO 💻🔌🍪💧

Who can participate?

While we've sent out special invites to some schools, any middle or high schooler in the Bay Area Peninsula is totally welcome to join the PantherHacks party! 🌉 Spots go like hotcakes, so don't wait – grab yours ASAP! 🎉🐾🔥

How will my project be scored?

At PantherHacks, we've got a theme and various prize categories to spice things up! Your project's score will be based on how well it rocks these elements. Plus, we're giving props for technical difficulty, so don't hold back – challenge yourself! 💪🏆🚀
